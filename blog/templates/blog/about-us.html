About Us


Benni Rugs

Welcome to Benni Rugs, where tradition meets innovation in the world of handcrafted rugs. Established in 1988, our journey began with a passion for creating exquisite, high-quality rugs that reflect both timeless elegance and contemporary design.

Our Story

For over three decades, Benni Rugs has been dedicated to the art of rug making. Founded by artisans who recognized the beauty and cultural significance of handwoven rugs, we have grown into a trusted name in luxury rugs. Our commitment to preserving traditional techniques while embracing modern aesthetics sets us apart in the industry.

Craftsmanship and Quality

At Benni Rugs, every rug is a masterpiece. Our skilled weavers use age-old techniques passed down through generations, ensuring each piece is unique and meticulously crafted. We source the finest materials, from premium wool to luxurious silk, to create rugs that are not only beautiful but also durable and long-lasting.

Design and Innovation

While we honor the traditions of rug making, we are also passionate about innovation. Our design team draws inspiration from various cultures and contemporary trends to create collections that appeal to diverse tastes. Our Mission

Our mission at Benni Rugs is to bring the timeless beauty of handcrafted rugs into homes around the world. We believe that a rug is more than just a floor covering; it's an expression of art and a centerpiece that can transform a space. We strive to provide our customers with rugs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also of the highest quality.

Sustainability and Ethics

We are committed to ethical practices and sustainability. We work closely with our artisans to ensure fair wages and safe working conditions. Additionally, we prioritize eco-friendly materials and processes to minimize our environmental impact.
